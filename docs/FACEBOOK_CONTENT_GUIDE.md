### Short Description (255 characters)
Kenya's trusted election management platform connecting voters with candidates. Follow campaigns, participate in polls, and track real-time election results. Available on Web, Mobile & USSD.

### Long Description (About Section)
**myVote Kenya** is Kenya's comprehensive election management platform that bridges the gap between electoral candidates and voters across all 47 counties.

**For Voters:**
✅ Follow your favorite candidates and political parties
✅ Participate in opinion polls in your electoral area
✅ Access candidate profiles, manifestos, and campaign updates
✅ Track real-time election results from your polling station
✅ Stay informed with verified electoral information

**For Candidates:**
✅ Build your political presence and reach voters
✅ Manage campaign agents across polling stations
✅ Send bulk communications to followers
✅ Track voter engagement and analytics
✅ Coordinate election day operations

**Platform Access:**
🌐 Web: www.myvote.ke

📞 USSD: Dial *384*VOTE#

**Coverage:**
🗳️ All 46,229 polling stations in Kenya
📍 290 constituencies across 47 counties
🏘️ 1,450 electoral wards

**Contact Us:**

Making Kenyan elections transparent, accessible, and engaging for everyone.
